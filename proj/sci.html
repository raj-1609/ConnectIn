Career options for job oriented students


1.DIPLOMA IN MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

Diploma in Mechanical Engineering is a 3 year polytechnic course for students after class 10th. Diploma in Mechanical Engineering deals with design, manufacture and maintenance of mechanical equipment through the application of science.

Mechanical Diploma or Mechanical Engineering polytechnic courses are offered to the candidates immediately after their 10th or equivalent exam. The eligibility for Mechanical diploma courses are a minimum score of 55% marks in class 10. A relaxation of 5% is provided to the candidates belonging to the reserved categories.

Diploma in Mechanical Engineering syllabus consists of the different subjects such as Fluid Mechanics and Machinery, Manufacturing Technology-I, Theory of Machines, Fluid Power Engineering, etc.

Top Diploma in Mechanical Engineering Colleges in India include Jamia Milia University, Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed University, CV Raman Global University etc The average course fees range from INR 10,000 to INR 2 lacs.

Diploma in Mechanical Engineering: Quick Facts

•The duration of the Diploma in Mechanical Engineering is 3 years.
•The candidates can pursue Diploma in Mechanical Engineering immediately after their 10th entrance exam.
•The entry to Mechanical engineering polytechnic course is either based on merit list or entrance exam.
•The top entrance exams for Diploma in Mechanical engineering are JEECUP, JEXPO, Odisha DET, etc.
•The top recruiters for the Mechanical engineering Diploma courses are Bajaj, Ford, Honda, NTPC, BHEL Hindustan Unilever, L&T, Ashok Leyland, etc.
•Candidates who have high aptitude and interest in machines, advanced mechanics, robotics should pursue this course.
•Candidates after completion of the course are accepted in different job profiles such as Mechanical Engineer, Manufacturing Manager, Sanction Head, Sales Engineer, Mechanical Technician, etc.
•The average salary of Mechanical Diploma Holders range between INR 20000-INR 500000.


2.Diploma in Civil Engineering

Diploma in Civil Engineering is a 3 year course for students after completion of Class 10 that teaches students to plan, design, execute and maintain structural works like bridges, buildings, roads and other infrastructural projects.

Diploma in Civil Engineering eligibility is students have to qualify 10th class with a minimum of 50% marks from a recognized institute.

Diploma in Civil Engineering admission process is to clear entrance exams taken by colleges to enrol students. The entrance exams are Delhi CET, AP JEE, Punjab PET, Odisha DET etc.

The average fee for the course diploma in civil engineering is INR 10,000-5,00,000. Fees structures made by colleges and universities on the basis of course offered by them.

The average annual salary for freshers is INR 3-20 LPA. Skills and experience are two important factors for a good salary package.


3.Diploma in Electrical Engineering

Diploma in Electrical Engineering is a 3 year skill enabling engineering programme that students can do after completing class 10 which deals with installation, maintenance, troubleshooting, magnetism, control systems, signal processing and telecommunications.

Diploma in Electrical Engineering after 10th from a recognised education board provides theoretical knowledge about real-world electrical problems involving various subjects like Microcontroller, Transducers, Sensors, and Motors etc.

Diploma in Electrical Engineering syllabus include Electrical Machines, Elements of Electrical Engineering, Embedded System, Transmission and Distribution, Electrical Installation, etc.

Diploma in Electrical Engineering eligibility is that a student must have passed class 10th board exams from a recognized state or central board with at least 50% with Science and Mathematics as compulsory subjects. Apart from this, some other requirements might be needed depending on college.

Diploma in Electrical Engineering fees varies between INR 50,000 to 1,50,000. There are numerous job opportunities for a student who has completed a Diploma in Electrical Engineering. Some of the job roles include - Electrical design engineer, Transform design engineer, Field application engineer, Technical trainer, Verification engineer, CAD engineer, etc.

After pursuing Diploma in Electrical Engineering, one can expect a maximum salary of INR 2.2 lakh/annum for the position of CAD Engineer. The average salary package for a fresher varies from INR 1.4-1.7 lakh.


4.Diploma in Computer Engineering

Diploma in Computer Engineering course is a 3-year diploma level course. The minimum eligibility for which is 10th schooling with at least minimum aggregate marks.

The average tuition fee charged for this course is between INR 1 - 5 Lacs for a span of 3 years.

Admission to this course depends on candidate’s performance in their high school exams. Some institutes conduct entrance exam. Diploma in Computer Engineering course covers the basic topics from computer programming, operating system, networking etc. The course expects students with good communication skills.

The successful candidates can look for opportunities in areas like Manufacturing companies, telecommunication companies, financial companies, retail companies, agricultural industries etc. They can take up their job positions like programmer, system analyst, cloud architect among many others.
